Summary: The YAD-K8800 is the true workhorse — 2.5× faster, nearly 2× more precise with automatic color registration, and handles a much wider range of materials (including composite films). It's positioned as a flexible-packaging press, not just a label printer. The YA800 trades speed and precision for lower power draw (33 vs 175 kW), simpler operation, and flexibility with up to 10 color stations. Use the YAD for big production runs and complex multi-material jobs; the YA800 for short runs, simpler single-material work, or when the YAD is busy.
