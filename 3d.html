Summary: The YAD-K8800 is the true workhorse — 2.5× faster, nearly 2× more precise with automatic color registration, and handles a much wider range of materials including composite films up to 150 g/m². It’s positioned as a flexible-packaging press, not just a label printer. The YA800 trades speed and precision for simpler operation and flexibility with up to 10 color stations. Use the YAD for big production runs and complex multi-material jobs; the YA800 for short runs, simpler single-material work, or when the YAD is busy.
